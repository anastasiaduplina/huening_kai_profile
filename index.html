Huening Kai

South Korean singer, rapper, and dancer

Huening Kai is the youngest member of the K-pop group TXT, which stands for Tomorrow X Together, and is known for his powerful vocals and impressive dance skills.

Family

Hueningkai is the middle child of Brazilian-American China-based singer and TV personality, Nabil David Huening and his Korean ex-wife Jung Yeonju. His older sister, J'LEA, is a soloist and a former member of the girl group VIVA, while his younger sister, Huening Bahiyyih, is a member of the project girl group Kep1er.

Education

Hueningkai graduated from the Hanlim Arts High School on February 9, 2021.

Carier

On January 15, Hueningkai was revealed as the third member of Big Hit Entertainment's newest boy group TXT. He made his official debut with the group on March 4, 2019, with their debut mini-album, The Dream Chapter: Star.
